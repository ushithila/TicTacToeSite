Rules and Download

Like any game you heard of, Tic Tac Toe has its own set of rules!

The aim of the game is for you to be the first to get three in a row. This could be in either horizonital, vertical, or diagonal. Your opponent (the other player) would have to block you from getting three in a row and try to get three in a row before you!

With that said, download our recreation (in the zip file) and have fun!

Enjoy!!!!
